Vegetation Density is a Sign of Climate Change

People normally look to temperature changes, glaciers, and ocean levels for signs of climate change
but there is something else that reacts much faster to effects of climate change...

For each year, vegetation density changes with the season

Becoming more dense and less dense according to the season

In a perfect world, the vegetation density would stay relatively stable for every year...

...but because of climate change vegetation density has changed throughout the years
