Vegetation Density is a Sign of Climate Change

Plants reacts to climate change before us humans do.
Effects of climate change---changes in rainfall, temperature, and CO2 levels---affect plants
much faster than other signs of climate change we are looking at.
Unlike ocean levels or melting glaciers that take years or decades to see changes,
changes in vegetation density can be seen within a single year.

For each year, vegetation density changes with the season
